The Drive Behind the Mission

With the sudden situation of a global pandemic, traveling has been greatly reduced. Because of this, campuses are not receiving the usual amount of prospective students roaming their buildings and greenery. It became obvious that there was a serious lack in the availability of virtual tours that can be either completed from the security of your own home or without the need of a tour guide present. With this application, this hole has been filled. Now interactions can be reduced as much as possible while still getting the personalized tour experience.
